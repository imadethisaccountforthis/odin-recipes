Ahi Tuna Poke

Description

Ahi Tuna Poke is a Hawaiian dish made from cubes of raw tuna and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of fresh tuna steaks
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place tuna in a medium non-reactive bowl. Add soy sauce, green onions, sesame oil, and sesame seeds; mix well. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
  3. Serve over rice
