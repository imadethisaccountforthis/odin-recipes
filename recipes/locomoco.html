Loco Moco

Description

Loco Moco is a Hawaiian comfort food dish. This dish is prepared by combining a burger patty and fried egg over a base of rice and covering them with gravy. Additionally, this dish is often served with macaroni salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 ground beef patties (4 ounces each)
  • 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 and a half cups beef stock
  • 4 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of worcester sauce
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 drops sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced green onions, white and light green parts only
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups hot cooked white rice
